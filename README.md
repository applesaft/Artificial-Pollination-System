# Hybrid Artificial Pollination System

The global decline of pollinators presents a significant threat to biodiversity and ecological stability. While conventional conservation strategies at national and international levels have attempted to address this crisis, solutions integrating technological advancements, including the use of Artificial Intelligence, are being explored. 

The project focuses on the development of a non-invasive and hybrid artificial pollination system. The project aims at contributing to a potential solution to the pollination crisis.

## Remarks
